Tic-Tac-'Rona!'

How to Play

In a classic game of tic-tac-toe, players take turns marking up a game board with X's or O's. The goal is to have three of your matching marks in a row, in order to win against the opposing player. Let's see what you've got against the Novel Coronavirus!
